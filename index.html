Pocket Wiki is a simple article creator project made by Weston. It follows a similar syntax to the Wikitext/Wiki Markup [1] . When you create an article it is as simple as pressing the Download button and uploading it wherever you would like to publish it. Pocket Wiki does not require any accreditation for usage, nor does it claim any ownership of the articles written on it.
